5 Great Reasons To Play Netball

And why you should join our league

Meet Like-Minded People

The people you will meet at these games all enjoy netball, so you already have something in common! As you attend more games, you could build a solid rapport with your teammates and other players that you will encounter during the league.

Exercise

Netball can be a fast-paced sport, meaning depending on the position you play, you will be very active during the game. This is great, as we all need 60 minutes of exercise a day to stay healthy! Keep active with us and join our league.

INDOOR AND OUTDOOR COURTS

We have 15 courts that we manage across Havering, 7 indoor and 8 outdoor. All courts come provided with umpires for booked games and a selection of refreshments to keep players hydrated and ready to play. If needed, we can provide bibs and balls for teams that don't have their own equipment. However, our hire kits are limited in supply, so you will need to let us know in your booking form if you require equipment. To avoid disappointment, we do recommend all teams to buy their own kit, which you can buy here.
